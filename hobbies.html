My hobbies

My first hobby

My first hobby was cooking/baking, especially baking, it was my calling, it all started when i had just finished JSS 2 and i went for summer school at St. Catherine high school (i think that was the name of the school) and we had to do catering and crafts, on that day, the teachers had decided we'd be baking vanilla cupcakes and chin-chin (a popular nigerian treat), we also had to do some crafts but that's a story for another day.

So when i had finished with my craft, i saw a group, struggling to decide who was going to mix the cake batter, i was intrigued, so i decided to join, i watched as they whipped the butter and sugar together, by hand too, adding all the dry ingredients and kneading the dough for the chin-chin, the whole experience was like discovering a new world for 12 year old me.

After that day, i kept on pressuring my mom to buy me the materials to learn how to bake and surprisingly my mom who would usually need a lot of convincing answered me rather easily. The first thing i made was the chin-chin, which i made under the supervision of my paternal grandmother and they came out amazing, the next thing i made was a vanilla cake and it did not come out as good as the chin-chin, turns out my beginner's luck had run out, the cake was not the worst thing i'd ever eaten it was just really dense and overmixed. At the time i thought i was unstoppable and that there was nothing i couldn't bake, i definitely thought wrong.

From then on, i just kept on honing the craft and i did eventually get better, in fact, i was so immersed in it that i created my very own recipe book, which i lost unfortunately, i'm sure you would've loved to see it.
